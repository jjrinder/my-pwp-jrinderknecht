"So encourage each other and build each other up, just as you are already doing."  ~ 1 Thess. 5:11

  We are 7th through 12th grade students that are part of First Baptist Church of West Albuquerque. Situated in the Taylor Ranch community, our goal is to be a place where students feel valued.

  Even more importantly, we want to present Jesus as he is so that each student will continually fall more deeply in love with him.

Sundays

Sundays at First West start with worship as a church at 9:30 a.m.

At 11 a.m. students gather in the youth room to connect, before separating into smaller groups (age and gender-specific) for Bible-centered discussion about life’s challenges.

Sundays Mornings at 9:30, and 11 to 12

Mid-week Bible Study

During the school-year, students are invited to meet mid-week for a time of Bible-study.

This semester, we are taking an in-depth look at God’s deliverance of the Israelites from slavery as told in the book of Exodus.

Wednesdays Evenings from 6:30 to 7:45

Game Night

One Friday a month, we hang out at the church and play a variety of games: card-games, foosball, the Wii...

Pizza is provided.

2nd Friday of the month, from 6 p.m. to 8 p.m.

Summer Camp

Student Camp is five days at Inlow Baptist Camp in the beautiful Manzano Mountains.

It is the perfect place to get away from everyday life, worship God, and connect with others.
