Milestone Two

Content Strategy

The site will be one page and consists of the following sections:

Welcome: A six to twelve word statement that welcomes people to the site. An image will be used as the background to give the user an immediate look at the organization. Additionally, the welcome section will include a menu bar that allows the user to jump to a specific organizational activity.

Who We Are: A short paragraph describing the organization. The background will be simple so that it does not become a distraction.

Activities: This section will have multiple sub-sections; all styled in a similar manner. Each sub-section will focus on a specific type of activity and will include a photo and a few details about the activity (general times, location, and a brief description)

Upcoming Events: The upcoming events section will list specific dates for approaching activities. This will allow users to get specific information on whether a weekly or monthly activity will be happening as planned.
