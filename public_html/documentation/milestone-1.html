Purpose

Audience

The organization creates a safe space for middle-school and high-school students to connect together in Northwest Albuquerque. The site is designed for all stakeholders within the community. That includes teenagers who are active members of the group, potential new participants, parents of said participants, and volunteers.

Goal

The primary goal of the site is to provide up-to-date information about organizational events. A secondary goal is to open up an additional channel with parents of participants. Teenagers often do not communicate well with their parents, but the site allows parents to receive organizational information on their own terms which hopefully will allow for them to feel at ease with the group.
